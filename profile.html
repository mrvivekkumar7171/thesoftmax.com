Vivek Kumar in a Nutshell

I am a data scientist with a passion for storytelling. I believe that words and data are the two most powerful tools to change the world. Most of my time is spent staring at a computer screen—programming, analyzing large datasets, building machine learning models, and delivering business insights. I enjoy challenges, solve Leetcode problems, and write tutorials on what I learn.


My journey began with exploring multiple domains like cybersecurity, web development, and Android development, and it eventually led me to the world of AI/ML. I feel fulfilled when I solve business problems using math and statistics. I believe I’m made for this.


I’ve completed 10+ data science projects, including 4 major end-to-end ones. My learning has been largely self-driven through platforms like Udemy, 365DataScience, CampusX, Coursera, and FreeCodeCamp. I’m grateful to the educators who make this knowledge accessible.
